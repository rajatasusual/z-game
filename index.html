  • The goal is to combine letters by sliding them in any direction and forming valid words.
  • Two tiles with the same letter combine into the next letter (e.g., A + A = B).
  • Earn points by forming valid 3-5 letter words based on the Scrabble point system.
  • Each new letter added to the board grants additional seconds based on its Scrabble point value.
  • No movement during a turn results in a -1 point penalty.
  • Matched words will disappear once clicked on, and the grid will shift to fill the gaps. Or you can continue playing the board.
  • You can only play each word once.
